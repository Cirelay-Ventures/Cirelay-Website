Security And Data Practices

Seller data is handled by scope, permission, and retention limit.

For engagements involving Seller Central or marketplace data, Cirelay applies access controls and retention practices designed around Amazon SP-API and marketplace data-protection expectations.

Credential management

  • Seller grants role-based access from the marketplace account.
  • Cirelay does not ask sellers to share passwords or MFA codes.
  • Access is reviewed quarterly for ongoing retainers and removed after the engagement ends.
  • Any secrets required for approved integrations are stored encrypted and limited to authorized personnel.

PII and retention

  • Buyer/order PII is used only for authorized account-management tasks.
  • Buyer/order PII is deleted or anonymized no later than 30 days after order delivery when order data is used, unless a longer period is legally required.
  • Non-PII operational records are retained only as long as needed for reporting, support, legal, or security purposes.
  • Deletion requests can be sent to hello@cirelay.com.

Network and device controls

  • HTTPS/TLS is used for web access and data transfer.
  • Administrative access requires MFA where supported.
  • Workstations used for seller work are protected with OS updates, device lock, and anti-malware controls.
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorized operators.

Incident response

  • Suspected security incidents are logged, triaged, contained, and investigated.
  • Affected sellers and marketplaces are notified when required by contract, law, or platform policy.
  • Corrective actions are documented and reviewed before service resumes.
  • Security contact: hello@cirelay.com.

The full policy, including collection, use, sharing, retention, incident response, and deletion practices, is available on the Privacy and Data Handling Practices page.

Engagement Process

Four steps before any account access is requested.

Every engagement starts with a written scope so the seller knows exactly why access is needed, what will be changed, and how results will be reported.

Scope and access plan

We define the marketplace, account area, requested permissions, expected deliverables, and data that may be accessed.

Baseline account review

We review account health, catalog/listing readiness, inventory workflow, and open platform notices based on seller-approved access.

Execution and documentation

We complete approved tasks, keep an action log, and request seller approval before sensitive account changes.

Weekly report and handoff

The seller receives status, completed work, risks, and recommended next actions. Access is reduced or removed when no longer needed.
